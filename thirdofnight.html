Night Prayers

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a simple, accurate, and ad-free prayer companion for Muslims worldwide. We aim to help users stay connected to their night worship, with a special focus on Midnight and the Last Third of the Night. The app is built to facilitate a smooth and spiritual end to the day by reciting Surah Al-Mulk and keeping track of the most blessed times of the night.

Application Goals

Night Prayers was created to address the need for a privacy-first, distraction-free application that focuses solely on providing accurate prayer times for Midnight and the Last Third of the Night. Our goal is to help Muslims strengthen their spiritual connection before sleep and maintain the important practice of reciting Surah Al-Mulk.

Privacy Policy

At Night Prayers, we prioritize your privacy. We collect minimal information, such as your city selection, to provide accurate prayer times. This data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore and is only used to improve the core functionality of the app. We do not share or sell your information to third parties. For donations, payment details are handled externally via Ko-fi.
