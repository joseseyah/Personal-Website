Welcome to Third of Night

Third of Night is an app designed to help Muslims track prayer times, particularly the last third of the night when special prayers are recommended.

The app uses your device's location to accurately determine prayer times based on your current location.

How Third of Night Helps Muslims

The app provides:

  • Accurate prayer times based on your location.
  • Reminders for important prayer timings, such as Fajr and Maghrib.
  • Information about the last third of the night, a special time for supplication and reflection.

Privacy Policy

Third of Night respects your privacy and only uses your location data to provide accurate prayer times. We do not collect or store any personal information.

Your location data is used solely for the purpose of calculating prayer times and is not shared with any third parties.
