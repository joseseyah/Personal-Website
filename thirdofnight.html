Third of the Night

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a simple, accurate, and ad-free prayer companion for Muslims worldwide. Stay connected to your night worship, focusing on Midnight and the Last Third of the Night. The app also provides the London Unified Prayer Time for those who prefer it, with no need for location settings.

Application Goals

Third of the Night focuses on delivering accurate prayer times for Midnight and the Last Third of the Night, helping users maintain the spiritual practice of reciting Surah Al-Mulk before sleep. The app also offers prayer notifications 10 minutes before each prayer, ensuring you never miss a blessed time.

Privacy Policy

We collect minimal data (like your city selection) to ensure accurate prayer times. Your privacy is our priority, and data is securely stored with no sharing or selling to third parties.
